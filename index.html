About Kosovo

Kosovo is a small, landlocked country in the Balkans with rich cultural heritage, breathtaking landscapes, and a fascinating history.

History of Kosovo

Kosovo has a rich and diverse history, influenced by the Roman, Ottoman, and modern European eras. It declared independence in 2008.

Culture
